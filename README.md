## Why Shipping to Saudi Arabia Is Complicated

Key reasons:
- Strict document matching
- SABER / SASO compliance
- Importer responsibility if not DDP
- High inspection rate for new sellers

Recommendation:
For Amazon & Noon sellers, DDP reduces operational risk.

## Air vs Sea Freight for Saudi Arabia (DDP)

Air freight:
- Faster
- Higher cost
- Best for product launch

Sea freight:
- Lower cost
- Longer transit
- Best for replenishment

Many Amazon sellers use a hybrid strategy.
